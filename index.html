OVERVIEW

Our Vision

The Etudiant Solar Garden House will be recognized by the communities as the provider of choice for solar-powered smart garden solutions that are the most innovative, efficient, eco-friendly, and best in the world.

Our Mission

To create innovative, eco-friendly, solar-powered smart garden solutions that simplify plant care, conserve resources, and inspire sustainable practices.

SWOT Analysis

S

Strengths

  • Promotes renewable energy use, reducing dependency on fossil fuels.
  • Lower long-term costs with reduced electricity and water bills.
  • Use of IoT and smart systems enhances gardening efficiency.

W

Weaknesses

  • Solar energy efficiency may decline during cloudy or rainy conditions.
  • Requires technical knowledge for installation and troubleshooting.
  • Older gardeners or non-tech-savvy users may face challenges adapting.

O

Opportunities

  • Rising awareness of climate change fuels demand for eco-friendly practices.
  • Opportunity to tap into urban farming, vertical gardens, and rooftop gardening markets.
  • Emerging AI solutions for predictive plant care and automated monitoring.

T

Threats

  • Traditional, less expensive gardening methods might appeal to some users.
  • Limited affordability in regions with lower economic development.
  • Dependence on local government regulations and renewable energy policies.
